Agnes Stenlund

Hello, I'm Agnes.


I'm currently designing things with the guys at Marvel in London and recently finished studying Interactive Art Director at Hyper Island in Stockholm (where I'm from). Before I got into design, I worked as a photographer.

I love to solve some hard problems together with thoughtful, pragmatic and curious people. I geek out on most parts of the design process; asking the right questions, understanding users, prototyping, the visual design (I love details), and writing the code.

I think a lot and usually out loud. When I don't design I like to talk about philosophy, data, and how the two may be combined. I also really like falafel.
