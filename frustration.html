Frustration Station

Concept / Code / Design

Frustration Station was a result of a project where the aim was to find new solutions for everyday objects, with the use of technology. is a way to ease mental stress, a frustration outlet. It is just a normal box with magic on the inside.

Frustration Station helps you deal with mental stress.

How it works: The box is equipped with light strips, led lights, sensors and speakers. You activate these by screaming as loud as you can, the sound sensor will register your frustrations and for ca 30 seconds you will be surrounded by sparkling stars and planets, calming music and light. It's now time to enjoy a few moments and connect with your senses. Within just a couple of seconds you’ll be blissfully relaxed. After 30 seconds the box goes back to idle mode and you're ready for the real world outside the box again.

Building The frustation station was a playful way of exploring the possibilities technology. When the prototype was designed, programmed and built, we exhibited our design and the response was positive. We learned that everyone needs to scream once in a while.
